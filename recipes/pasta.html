My own One Pan Pasta recipe

Return to hompage

Description

Delicious and simple recipe in 15mins for 4 servings!

Ingredients:

  • one packet of pasta
  • salt and pepper
  • one diced clove of garlic
  • two diced tomatoes
  • one slice of lemon
  • 200g of diced chicken or prawns
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Pour generous amount of olive oil on medium heat, sauteed garlic till golden brown
  2. Put in tomatoes and season with salt and pepper, cook for 2mins
  3. Pour 1 litre of boiling water in the same pot and cook pasta for 8mins
  4. Once pasta is cooked, put the meat of your choice in and stir fry it until cooked
  5. Squeeze the lemon for the final touch and season salt and pepper as required
